```
p -> q     <=>  q' -> p'
p' v q     <=>  q' -> p'  Implication
q v p'     <=>  q' -> p'  Commutative
(q')' v p' <=>  q' -> p'  Double Negative
q' -> p'   <=>  q' -> p'  Implication
```

```
(p -> r) ^ (q -> r)     <=> (p v q) -> r
(p' v r) ^ (q' v r)     <=> (p v q)' v r         Implication
(p' v r) ^ (q' v r)     <=> (p' ^ 'q) v r        DeMorgan's
(p' v r) ^ (q' v r)     <=> (p' v r) ^ (q' v r)  Distributive
```
